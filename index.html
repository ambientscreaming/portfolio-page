about k.b.

artist, poet, silly goose

there's a customer who comes into my work, who all the girls refer to as "the guy who looks like he got struck by lightning". his hair is grey and stands up like someone rubbed a balloon on it, and he smells like a gas station toilet. i might feel bad for him if he weren't a customer in the place that he was, acting the way that he does. i digress.

the other day he phones one of the girls from work -- the only one who will sit with him. she sits with him for hours on end. i honestly don't know how she does it; between the smell and the personality, it's unfathomable to me. but she does. she does enough to have given him her phone number to arrange future hours of sitting, apparently. anyway, he phoned her and told her he fell in a puddle and that she was just going to have to "take him as he was" becuase he didn't have time to go home and change before seeing her. this seemed both absurd to me - a grown man falling into a puddle so far that he'd get soaking wet - and also like a blessing for him. maybe he'd smell better.

he got to the club. he looked electrocuted. she sat with him placating him in his deranged behavior. he left. she was drunk. same as it ever was. another staff member more forwardly curious than the rest of us asked her, "is he normal? or is he actually like, as mentally ill as he looks?" she replied back, "oh no, he's fully insane. today he told me when he dies he's going to come back as the sun and burn everyone on earth alive."
